Blending

Tea blending is the art of combining different teas, herbs, flowers, or spices to create a balanced and expressive flavor profile. It lets makers highlight the strengths of each ingredient while softening any harsh notes, resulting in a harmonious cup. Blending also encourages creativity, allowing you to design teas that match a mood, a season, or a personal preference—and why not start? Crafting your own blend lets you steep a tea that's truly personal—your mood, your story, your flavor.
